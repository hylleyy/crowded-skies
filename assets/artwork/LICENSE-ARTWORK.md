This section governs the use of the software and raw assets.

2. Fan Content Policy (The “Inkpunk Promise”)

We love seeing your creativity! We grant you permission to create derivative "Fan Content" (art, non-commercial mods, fan-sites) under the following objective conditions:
- **Non-Commercial**: Fan Content must be free to access. You may not sell items or mods that include or require our protected assets.
- **Attribution**: You must provide clear credit to Inkpunk Game Studio as the original creator.

3. Community Standards & Values

While the above rules are the legal “floor,” we ask our community to build upon the “ceiling” of our studio values. Inkpunk Game Studio reserves the right to revoke Fan Content permissions for works that violate these standards:
- **The Human Touch**: Our studio believes in the power of human expression. We strongly prioritize and encourage human-made art. We reserve the right to distance our brand from works primarily generated by AI models that do not align with our creative ethics.
- **Safety and Respect**: Permission is automatically revoked for any content that promotes hate speech, illegal activities, or discriminatory behavior. We maintain a zero-tolerance policy for content that harms the safety or integrity of our community.
- **Studio Discretion**: Inkpunk Game Studio remains the final arbiter of what constitutes a violation of these values. We reserve the right to request the removal of content that we deem, in good faith, to be harmful to the project’s reputation.

4. Enforcement

Any use of assets outside of the “Permitted Use” or “Fan Content Policy” requires prior written consent from Inkpunk Game Studio. Failure to comply with the Conditions in Section 2 or the Standards in Section 3 may result in a formal request for removal or legal action to protect our intellectual property and the project's integrity.
